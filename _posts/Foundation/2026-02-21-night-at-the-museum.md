## Highlights from Night at the Museum

Night at the Museum at Del Norte High School showcased many electives and student projects from the trimester. Inside the computer science classrooms, students presented what they built and explained their process. I also heard from CSA and CSP students about the challenges they faced, how they solved problems, and what they learned while creating their projects.

## Beyond the Computer Science Room

Outside the computer science area, more classrooms and open spaces featured work from other programs. Near the fine arts building, I saw displays like pottery, paintings, and other creative projects that showed the variety of opportunities students have across campus.
